Super Duper Wordle Game

How to Play

  1. Guess the five-letter hidden word in 6 tries.
  2. Type letters on your keyboard.
    Press Enter to submit, Backspace to erase.
  3. After submitting, tiles change colour:
    — correct letter & position
    — correct letter, wrong position
    — letter not in the word
  4. The server serves one “word of the day”, so everyone gets the same puzzle.
